Synopsis

A young, fun, adventurous Panda travels from China to Africa to rescue his best friend, Jielong the Dragon, who has been kidnapped. On his journey he discovers a strange but amazing new world of mountains, deserts and jungles, while facing frightening hippos, suspicious hyenas and wise gorillas.
